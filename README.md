☕ Daily Grind Coffee Dashboard
An end-to-end data analysis project covering 3 years of sales data (2023–2025) for a coffee brand across the United States, built using SQL, Python, and Power BI.
📌 Project Overview
This dashboard provides a comprehensive view of business performance across regions, products, and customers — transforming raw transactional data into actionable insights for decision-makers.
Dataset scope:
🗓️ Period: 2023 – 2025
📦 Products: 25 products across 5 categories
👥 Customers: 200 customers
🌍 Market: United States

🔍 Key Insights
📍 Regional Performance
West is the highest-revenue region at $113K, also leading in customer count with 81 customers (41%)
Despite this, the top individual customer came from the North, generating $8,904 in revenue across 30 orders

📦 Product Insights
Grinders & Brewers is the top-performing category with $504K in sales — 58% of total revenue
Overall profit margin: 55%

👥 Customer Behavior
200 customers with an average of 22 orders per customer over 3 years
This suggests an opportunity to improve customer retention and repeat-purchase strategies

🛠️ Tools & Technologies
Python: Data cleaning & preprocessing
SQL: Data querying & transformation
Power BI: Dashboard design & visualization

📊 Dashboard Pages
1. Overview: High-level KPIs including total revenue, profit margin, and regional performance breakdown.
2. Products: Analysis of 25 products across 5 categories, highlighting top performers by revenue and margin.
3. Customers: Customer segmentation by region, order frequency, and individual revenue contribution.
